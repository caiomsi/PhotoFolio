02 About
© IRIS VANCE
New York City, 2023
"I don't photograph
what I see —
I photograph
what I feel."

Based in New York City, I've spent over a decade pursuing the intersection of light, emotion, and narrative. My work spans editorial campaigns, fine art exhibitions, and intimate portraiture for those who believe images should outlast the moment they capture.

Every frame is a collaboration between the instant and the lens — a conversation between the seen and the felt.
