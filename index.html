02 About
© LUCAS CAMPO
Sideline, 2024
"I don't photograph
the match —
I photograph
the kids who live it."

I go to every pitch, every Sunday, with one goal: to freeze the moments these young players will talk about for the rest of their lives. The first goal, the last-minute save, the pile-on after a win — I'm there for all of it.

Youth football is raw, honest, and full of heart. My camera goes where the action is — no studio, no setups, just the game exactly as it happens.

6+ Years on the Pitch
